    • WHAT WE DO

      To empower poor and women economically and socially through organising networking establishing sustainable livelihood activities access to financial services and implement effectively and locally relevant poverty reduction measures and capacity building services. Leap Trust motive is to promote thrift and credit activities among the members of SHG/JLG and to improve their economic and social status.

      The specific objects are:

      To provide technical and expertise service to members in building their capacities and engage technical experts personnel therefore.

      To conduct training programs for the members in managing their affairs.

      To promote new entities FPO'S, social enterprises by subscription to equity or other suitable instruments in LEAP.

      To secure fund from other funding sources and provide same as loan with or without interest to the members so as to enable them to take up income generation and livelihood programs.

      To convene skill training ,meetings, conferences, seminars, workshops and exhibition to market support for achieving objectives of the LEAP.

      NABARD water conservation campaign

      NABARD water conservation campaign

      We coordinated NABARD Funded water conservation campaigns at 2017 in cuddalore district 75 panchayats at kammapuram and vridhachalam blocks,Farmer club members,SHG members,general public actively participated,during campaign preparing village level water resource mapping, selection of village volunteers selection,village water bodies to be desilted, cleaning and weeding of ponds had identified and reported to district administration through DDM.Importance of water conservation, rain water harvesting system, Micro-irrigation system, resorting to ground water recharging structures, Trees planting/ social foresting improved Agronomic practices for improving water use efficiency had explained about government schemes for creating water related structures and technologies( micro irrigation, sprinkler irrigation).Most of villages excellently participated and take part in our activities while campaign awareness like meeting,rally,pledge,resources maping and identifying action plan.
